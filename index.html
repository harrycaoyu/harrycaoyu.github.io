What it is

Brizi is an intelligent blimp equipped with a camera, that responds to its environment. It can be controlled both from a mobile device and by hand gestures, and makes footage instantly available to social applications.

Features

Brizi can fly for 2 hours.

Brizi uses Arduino.

Brizi exposes a RESTful HTTP API.
