Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Brizi different from a drone with a camera?

Today's most advanced and high-end drones can stay afloat in the air for up to 15 minutes, Brizi's proprietary lightweight system runs for 3 hours before needing a quick battery swap. Drones are controlled by a single person where Brizi is building intelligent systems that can respond to an entire crowd's interactions, allowing the audience to direct its flight path.

Where can I get my own Brizi?

Today, Brizi is only available for commercial use with large audiences. We work closely with event organizers and sponsors to bring the best event experience possible to big crowds. If you have an event you'd like to see Brizi at, Drop us a line!

What are the requirements I need to fly Brizi?

Brizi needs at least 18 foot open ceilings to fly indoors. The altitude is easily adjustable but normally it is set at 4 meters above the crowd.

How long does the battery last on Brizi?

Each flight lasts around 3 hours before a battery swap is required.

Can more than one Brizi fly at the same time?

Yes!

Does Brizi come in different sizes / colors / shapes?

We can customize the blimp envelope design in all sorts of ways to match your needs. We recommend the traditional prolate spheroid shape for maximum flight stability.

What safety measures have you taken with Brizi?

We take safety very seriously and make our envelopes out of the same material as beachballs, which lets air seep out very slowly when punctured and lets impact upon human contact be barely noticeable. We ensure the usage of non-flammable helium and flexible propellors, flying at no more than 0.5kph.

What measures have you taken in regards to privacy?

All events covered by Brizi are used at the event organizer's own discretion. We ask that attendees are made aware in writing there may be photography and videography taken on site. If you would like to dispute content published by Brizi, we're more than happy to assist you at hello@flybrizi.com.

What if I have another question?

Contact us at hello@flybrizi.com!
